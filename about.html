About

Fortino Dental is a comprehensive dental office conveniently located in beautiful Ancaster and serves the Ancaster, Dundas, Hamilton and surrounding communities. The office is operated by Dr. Joseph Fortino and his highly trained and professional staff. Fortino Dental strives to provide the best in general and cosmetic dentistry to the community by offering the most conservative and innovative options available in dentistry today.

Our brand new office is equipped with the latest in dental technology including digital x-rays, allowing us to be on the cutting edge in both comfort and care. Dr. Fortino and his staff work hard to make sure patients leave with a bright smile on their face, and maintain it for years to come.

Make an appointment with us and know you are in the hands of a caring, compassionate and skilled dental team.

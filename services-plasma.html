Platelet-rich Plasma

Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) is a new approach to tissue regeneration and it is becoming more valuable in promoting the of healing dental and oral surgery procedures. The use of PRP in surgical settings can be quite beneficial, as it reduces bleeding, promotes bone regeneration, and enhance soft tissue healing. Clinical studies have suggested that promising results have been yielded from the application of PRP in many dental and oral procedures (i.e. implant surgery, periodontal surgery, tooth extractions).

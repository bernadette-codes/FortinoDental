Lip Fillers

You might want to try lip fillers, which add or restore fullness to your lips, because:

  • your lips have lost definition over the years
  • you’re tired of using makeup to enhance your pout and want something longer lasting
  • you think that a more defined smile and shapelier lips would suit your face better

Getting lip fillers – which is also known as ‘lip enhancement’ or ‘lip augmentation’ – is relatively painless, and you’ll see the results immediately:

  • The treatment takes about 30 mins, and you may experience some slight swelling or bruising which varies for each person
  • You can choose the exact look you want, whether that’s a subtle change or a big difference
  • Lip fillers aren’t permanent and can be expected to last typically 6-12 months; you’ll need a top up after this period to maintain volume and definition

Our Lip Fillers

We use Juvederm which is the #1 doctor recommended facial filler in Canada and worldwide. Juvederm Volbella is a hyaluronic acid filler and a great product to choose for small fine lines and enhancement of the lips. The smaller particle size allows for the correction of these fine areas with a smooth and natural result.
